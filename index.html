Best Practices & Tips

There are many things you can do to make it through GA Web Development Immersive. The first thing is be prepared to work hard.

  1. Use your resources
    • Outcomes
    • Previous Cohort
    • Night Time TA
    • Online Community
    • Love Ones
  2. Share your success
    • Congratulate each other on your breakthroughs
  3. Encourage each other
    • try new things
    • if your hear a person giving up, tell them to keep going
    • nobody can know everything but together as a collective we can know a lot
  4. Have Fun
    • participate in events
    • make funny gifs
    • enjoy the process
  5. Stay Positive
    • it is really important to have a good outlook while your here
    • good vibes are contagious
    • being positive can rolls up hill and downhill :)
  6. Use Your Past Experience
    • using your previous work & life experience will aid your growth
    • Sales Experience
      • sell your ideas for applications
      • design your product with features you can sell
    • Customer Service Experience
      • caring about a customer
      • treat your classmates good
    • Education Experience
      • use your learning techniques with your classmates
    • Health Care Experience
      • promote healthy well beings with the group
    • Management Experience
      • use your time management skills to plan out projects
      • explain the big picture
    • Sports
      • promote teamwork
      • proper mental states to reach your goals
  7. Ask questions
    • be curious
  8. Fall in love with your process
    • there isn't a real destination but a journey
    • each step take you deeper down your path of being a developer but the path never ends
